About us

The Black Tech Union is a nonprofit initiative with the goal of fostering a community of professionals, organizations and universities who are serious, intentional and passionate about empowering people of African descent in technology careers and entreprenuership.

We develop partnerships with community organizations, universities and corporations to facilitate programs that drive a measurable increase in the representation of people from the African diaspora working in technology companies and funded startups.

We are directed by a volunteer team of proven industry professionals whom are always open and welcoming to all in our community and allies.

We are working to amplify the one "ism" that doesn't hurt our communtiy...Racism, Sexism, Ageism, Nepotism!
